I appreciate your thoughs on decriminalizing sex work; yet I believe that a sex worker is better off using his/her sensuality to create art; he or she is better off being an erotic artist.

Sex work should be decriminalized: punishing a sex worker is tantamount to punishing an obese person for eating too much sugary foods.

However, decriminalizing sex work does not help the sex worker's emotional health; just as decriminalizing drugs does not help the drug addict's emotional health..

As human beings, we are healthier when we say "no" to money and "yes" to art.

Art is essential to healing emotional, spiritual and psychological damage.

Because art can heal the human spirit, helping a sex worker, drug addict or traumatized individual create art is beneficial to that individual and to society at large.

Healing, nonetheless, is a continual, never-ending process; Jungian psychology can help us use art to become integrated individuals.

Individuation can help a sex worker become an erotic artist; the Erotic Artist can use his or her gifts for sensuality to create works of art that help him or her heal and become healthy.
