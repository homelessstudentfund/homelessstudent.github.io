Punishing a traumatized individual is tantamount to punishing an obese person for eating too much sugary foods.

As human beings, we are healthier when we say "no" to money and "yes" to art.

Art is essential to healing emotional, spiritual and psychological damage.

Because art can heal the human spirit, helping a sex worker, drug addict or traumatized individual create art is beneficial to that individual and to society at large.

Healing, nonetheless, is a continual, never-ending process; Jungian psychology can help us use art to become integrated individuals.
